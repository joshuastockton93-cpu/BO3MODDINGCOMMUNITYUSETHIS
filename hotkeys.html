Radiant Hotkeys

A complete list of essential shortcuts for faster mapping.

Movement

  • WASD — Move camera
  • Right‑click — Look around
  • Shift — Move faster

Selection

  • Ctrl + Left Click — Multi‑select
  • Alt + Left Click — Select face
  • Ctrl + I — Invert selection

Editing

  • X — Delete
  • Space — Duplicate
  • Shift + D — Toggle grid snapping
