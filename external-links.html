How This Site Uses External Links

This page explains how BO3 Modding Hub uses external links, what content is hosted elsewhere, and how ownership and rights are respected.

🔗 External Links Only

BO3 Modding Hub does not host or redistribute any third‑party files. All downloads, assets, scripts, models, FX, and tools linked on this website are hosted externally.

📦 Ownership of External Content

All external content remains the property of its original creators. This includes:

  • Prefabs
  • FX packs
  • Models
  • Materials
  • Scripts
  • Sound packs
  • Tools and utilities

BO3 Modding Hub does not claim ownership of any linked content.

🧩 Devraw.net Integration

Many links on this site point to Devraw.net, a community‑driven asset library. All Devraw content is credited to its respective authors and remains hosted on Devraw servers.

🛡 Why This Matters

This policy ensures:

  • Creators retain full ownership of their work
  • No copyrighted content is re‑uploaded
  • All downloads remain safe and verified
  • Your site stays compliant and cannot be taken down

📬 Contact for Removal Requests

If any creator wishes to have a link removed or updated, they may contact the site owner. Since no files are hosted here, removal is immediate and simple.
