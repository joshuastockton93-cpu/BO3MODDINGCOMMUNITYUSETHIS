Troubleshooting Tools

Log Analyzer

⚠ Log Analyzer (BETA)

This tool is still in active development. Results may not be fully accurate, and some BO3-specific errors may not yet be detected. Please report issues or false positives to help improve the analyzer.

The Log Analyzer helps you diagnose issues in your BO3 mod by scanning your console.log, linker logs, and compile output for common errors, missing assets, script issues, and warnings.

What It Detects

  • Missing models, images, materials, and sounds
  • Script compile errors and undefined functions
  • Broken asset paths in zone files
  • Radiant entity warnings
  • FX or sound alias loading failures
  • Memory or performance warnings

How It Works

Paste your log into the analyzer below and it will automatically highlight errors, warnings, and potential fixes. This tool is designed to help new and experienced Map Makers quickly identify problems without digging through thousands of lines manually.
